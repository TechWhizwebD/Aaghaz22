Swaragini

Indian Classical Choir Competition (Hybrid Mode)

Rules and Regulations:

1. The event is open to both UG and PG students from all over India. Max age limit for the participants is 23 years, as of 31st March 2022.
2. A team can have a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 12 vocalists along with a maximum of 2 accompanists on two different instruments.
3. A team member who can sing and play an instrument is considered as vocalist and accompanist both.
4. Only Indian Classical compositions are allowed.
5. There will be 2 rounds - Prelims and Finale.
6. The time limit for the whole composition is 13 minutes (3min for soundcheck + 10mins for composition) at max.
7. The details of the finale will be shared only with the selected teams.
8. The decision of judges shall be considered final and binding.
9. The results of the prelims will be shared only on the official Instagram handle of Raaga (@raaga.ims). So make sure to follow us on Instagram. 10. For prelims, the teams will have to submit their recorded performance along with their registrations on the Google Form.
11. The audio of the uploaded video should be clear with no noise or disturbances in the background. If the committee identifies any kind of manipulation with the audio and/or video, the entry will be disqualified without any notice or negotiation.
12. The deadline for registration is 2nd April 2022, Saturday, 11:59 PM. Deadlines are to be strictly adhered to.

Naad

Indian Solo Instrumental Competition (Online Mode)

Rules and Regulations:

1. This is a solo instrumental event with a single open round.
2. The event is open to both UG and PG students from all over India. Max age limit is 23 years, as of 31st March 2022.
3. The participating instrumentalists will be asked to record a video of themselves playing the instrument of their choice and upload them.
4. Participants can play any song or composition of their choice among the following genres - Bollywood, Sufi, folk, and semi-classical.

6. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
7. The results of the prelims will be shared only on the official Instagram handle of Raaga (@raaga.ims). So make sure to follow us on Instagram.

FANTASIA

THE WESTERN SOLO SINGING COMPETITION (HYBRID)

Rules for Prelims(Online)

There will be two rounds: Prelims and Finals.

1. Backing tracks/instrumentals are not allowed.
2. Accompanists and accompanying instruments are also not allowed.
3. The maximum duration of the performance is 3 minutes.
4. The video should be completely raw and unedited, with no special effects or edits. Doing this will lead to disqualification.
5. The songs should be in English language (Western). Rap and explicit songs are not allowed.
6. The deadline for the first round is 3 rd April, 2022. 7. Candidates shortlisted in the Prelims will be informed about the final round.
8. The results will be announced on the official Instagram Handle of Conundrum Collective (conundrum_collective).

Rules for Finals(Offline)

1. The final round will be an offline event.
2. Backing tracks/instrumentals are not allowed.
3. Maximum of ONE accompanist is allowed.
4. Maximum duration of the performance is 6 minutes including the sound check and introduction of the participant.
5. The performance will be judged on the basis of song selection, dynamics, pitch and overall impact.
6. Decision of the judges will be final and binding on all the participants.
7. Final event will take place on 5th April, 2022.
